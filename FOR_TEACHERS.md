## How to Get Started (3 Steps!)

### Step 1: Open the Builder
Open `teacher-builder.html` in your web browser (just double-click it!)

### Step 2: Fill Out the Form
- Choose what type of activity you want
- Describe what you're teaching
- Tell us what students should learn
- Select any special features

### Step 3: Use with AI
- Click "Generate My Prompt"
- Copy the prompt that appears
- Paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI assistant
- Get your complete activity ready to use!

## That's It!

The AI will create a complete HTML file that you can:
- Open in any web browser
- Share with students
- Print out if needed
- Save and use again later
