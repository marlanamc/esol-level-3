Where Do They Overlap?

Present Continuous

  • Focus: "I have a plan"
  • Planned/scheduled events
  • Arranged activities
  • What you're doing later

Future Continuous

  • Focus: "At this time, this will be happening"
  • Action in progress at a future time
  • What's happening at a specific moment
  • More about the ongoing action

Present Continuous

Think: "I have this scheduled"

"I'm meeting Susan tomorrow."
"We're flying to Paris next week."

Future Continuous

Think: "At this time, this will be happening"

"At 2 PM, I'll be meeting clients."
