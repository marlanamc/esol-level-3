About The Liberty Hotel Boston

The Liberty Hotel is a luxury landmark in Boston's historic Beacon Hill. Originally a historic site, it has been transformed into a premier destination for world-class hospitality. We offer an environment where history meets modern luxury, and we are dedicated to providing our guests with an unforgettable experience.

Job Summary

We are looking for a professional and enthusiastic Hotel Supervisor to join our leadership team. You will be responsible for supervising staff, ensuring the highest level of guest satisfaction, and maintaining our reputation for excellence in the Boston luxury market.

Responsibilities

  • Supervise hotel staff and oversee daily operations in the front-of-house areas.
  • Support front desk and concierge employees to ensure smooth check-ins and check-outs.
  • Resolve guest complaints and issues with a calm, professional, and helpful attitude.
  • Train and guide new employees on hotel policies and service standards.
  • Ensure guest rooms and common areas meet our strict quality and cleanliness standards.
  • Communicate clearly with senior management and the entire hotel team.
  • Maintain a professional appearance and lead by example at all times.

Required Skills

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills (English proficiency required).
  • Proven leadership ability and experience in a supervisory role.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work under pressure.
  • Superior organization and time management skills.
  • Extensive customer service experience, preferably in hospitality.
  • Professional appearance and behavior suitable for a luxury hotel.

Preferred Experience

  • Previous experience in the hospitality industry is highly preferred.
  • Experience leading a team or supervising staff in a fast-paced environment.
  • Experience in handling and resolving customer complaints.
  • Multilingual ability is a significant advantage.

Compensation

  • Salary range: $62,000-$78,000 per year, based on experience.
  • Potential additional pay: annual performance bonus and holiday pay.
  • Final offer depends on experience, schedule flexibility, and interview performance.

Personal Qualities

The ideal candidate is responsible, reliable, friendly, and highly organized. You must be able to stay calm during busy shifts and possess a passion for providing outstanding service to our guests.
