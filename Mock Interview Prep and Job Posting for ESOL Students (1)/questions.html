A

Say: "Hi, I'm ____. I am the Front Desk Manager here at the Liberty Hotel. I will be interviewing you as part of your panel interview today."

  • 1. Tell me about yourself.
  • 2. Why did you leave your last job?
  • 3. What does good customer service mean to you?
  • 4. Tell me about a time you helped an unhappy guest.
  • 5. How do you handle a problem with a coworker when it is very busy?
  • 6. When can you work? Include weekends and holidays.

F

Say: "Hi, I'm ____. I am the Operations Manager here at the Liberty Hotel. I will be interviewing you as part of your panel interview today."

  • 1. Tell me about yourself.
  • 2. What did you do in your last job? What were your main duties?
  • 3. How do you stay organized when it is very busy?
  • 4. Tell me about a time you had many urgent tasks. How did you decide what to do first?
  • 5. How do you handle a problem with a coworker when you have to finish work on time?
  • 6. Why do you want to work here? Why now?

Instructions: Start with a handshake and greeting. Ask all six questions. Ask at least one follow-up question. Listen carefully. Keep each interview 6-8 minutes. Make eye contact. Do not write.
