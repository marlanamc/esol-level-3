Sentence Builder Concept Cards

Print this page to create concept cards for sentence building activities.

All cards are 2.4" × 2.0" (slightly larger than sentence builder cards) and can be cut out along the borders.

Printing Instructions: Cards are arranged with fronts first, then backs. Print each page with fronts on one side and backs on the reverse.

Double-Sided Printing: When printing double-sided, make sure to select "Flip on short edge" in your printer settings. The backs are automatically reversed to align correctly.
