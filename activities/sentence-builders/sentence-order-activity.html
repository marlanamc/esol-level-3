Sentence Order Activity Slide 1

Put the Sentences in Order

Instructions: Order each sentence using: WHO → VERB → WHAT → WHERE → WHEN
Remember: WHERE and WHEN can go at the beginning with a comma!
Not all sentences have all components.
  • 1 She eats pizza at home every day.
  • 2 Yesterday, I studied English.
  • 3 They are playing soccer in the park now.
  • 4 At 3pm, he was reading a book in the library.
  • 5 Tomorrow, we will visit the museum.
  • 6 She will be cooking dinner tonight.
  • 7 She doesn't live here.
  • 8 In the park, they aren't playing.
  • 9 Yesterday, I didn't study.
  • 10 At 3pm, he wasn't reading in the library.
  • 11 Tomorrow, we won't visit.
  • 12 She has eaten pizza at home every day.
Sentence 1 - Answer Slide 2

Sentence 1: Simple Present

Simple Present Tense
Formula:
WHO + VERB + WHAT + WHERE + WHEN
She eats pizza at home every day
She eats pizza at home every day.

Sentence 2: Simple Past

Simple Past Tense
Formula:
WHEN , WHO + VERB + WHAT

Yesterday, I studied English.
