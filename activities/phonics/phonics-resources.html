üí° Teaching Tips for Spanish-Speaking Adult Learners:

üá™üá∏ Compare with Spanish
Always contrast with Spanish: "In Spanish, B and V sound the same (/b/). In English, V is /v/ with teeth on lip."
ü¶∑ TH Sound Practice
For TH, have students put tongue between teeth and blow gently. Practice "this/that" vs "sis/sat" contrasts.
üëÖ R Sound Training
English R is made at back of mouth (not rolled like Spanish RR). Practice saying "ruh" with tongue back.
üó£Ô∏è Minimal Pairs Focus
Use minimal pairs: ship/sheep, bit/beat, bet/bat. Spanish speakers struggle with these vowel differences.
üé≠ Mouth Position Mirror
Have students watch themselves in a mirror. For W, show how lips round more than Spanish "u".
üîÑ Transfer to Words
Connect sounds to words immediately: "THis" ‚Üí "this", "THat" ‚Üí "that". Build confidence with familiar words.
